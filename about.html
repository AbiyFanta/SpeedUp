How Does it Work?

SpeedUp uses the Google Maps Platform API to create an app capable of determining the optimal route, expected ETA, and ETA depending on your speed. (may or may not be above speed limit :3)

1) Retreive user's starting location and destination

2) Use Google Map's API to determine the distance travelled and the expected ETA.

3) From the user-inputted speed, determine the difference in ETA.
